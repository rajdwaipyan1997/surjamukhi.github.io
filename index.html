About Surjamukhi Sangstha

A leading NGO working for community development since 1983

Since 1983

Surjamukhi Sangstha (SMS) is a well-established non-governmental organization dedicated to the socio-economic empowerment of poor and disadvantaged people, with particular emphasis on women and ethnic minorities across Bangladesh.

Led by Ms. Ashalata Baidya, our organization works extensively across 42 upazilas under 18 districts, serving approximately 275,000 families through various development programs including training, education, micro-credit, and healthcare support.

With a history dating back to 1983, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in community development, working closely with local communities, government agencies, and international organizations to create sustainable change.
